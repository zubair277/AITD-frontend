What You Will Learn

How to learn WhatsApp & Instagram marketing for Indian customers.

How to design smart incentives for your sales team, no discount wars.

How to expand into booming Tier-2/Tier-3 markets.

How to turn 'jugaad' selling into value-based selling.

How to upsell to existing customers who already trust you.

How to partner with distributors, kirana stores, e-commerce – expand without new shops.

How to brand your product right (packaging, positioning) – stop competing only on price.

How to build a simple sales dashboard – know your sales, no manual updates needed.

Maximize Profits

How to find bank loans, NBFCs, angels – stop relying only on family money.

How to negotiate better with suppliers, buy in bulk, or team up with other businesses.

How to know your real product cost (all hidden expenses) – stop selling at a loss.

How to go digital for payments – get discounts, reduce cash leakage.

How to improve collections – get customers to pay on time.

How to use government schemes, subsidies, MSME benefits – lighten your financial load.

Prevent Leakages

How to set up a simple CRM (even Google Sheets!) to never lose a lead.

How to use CCTV + simple software (Tally, Zoho, Vyapar) to stop pilferage.

How to map your processes (even on paper!) to find where time and money are wasted.

How to implement 'Indian-style' lean principles – reduce waste, reworks, late deliveries.

How to track inventory digitally, ditch 'Munimji’s diary'.

Business Automation

How to manage non-core work (delivery, accounting, HR) – focus on your core.

How to hire trustworthy managers without fear of them stealing customers or starting rivals.

How to set clear SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) – staff runs daily operations, no 'Sir, ab kya karein?' calls.

How to structure your team (org chart) – clear roles, no everyone reporting to 'Bauji'.

How to use AI tools (ChatGPT, Zapier, Indian apps) to automate billing, stock, customer replies.

How to professionalize hiring – move beyond 'bhai ka dost' recruitment.

How to get a professional CEO, set his pay, build checks – protect your business.

How to design a simple report (MIS dashboard) – get a business snapshot in 10 minutes.

How to create decision rules – every small choice doesn't need your approval.

How to take a 2-week holiday and find your business still standing.

How to slowly shift from being an operator → owner → investor in your own business.

Family Harmony

How to write a clear will and succession plan – avoid court battles for your children.

How to build a family constitution – rules for who joins, who gets paid, how conflicts are solved.

How to train the next generation right – teach them your business before giving control.

How to separate 'ghar ki baatein' (family drama) from 'business ki baatein' (strategy, money).

How to create fair salaries for family members – prevent resentment.

How to bring in independent advisors/mentors – mediate fights when egos clash.

How to resolve ego clashes between brothers/cousins – clear roles, decision rights.

How to set up a family council – resolve disputes before they hit the shop floor.

How to balance tradition (loyalty) with professionalism.

How to avoid 'sab kuch mera hai' mindset – build a legacy beyond one generation.

Three Stage Execution Framework

Stage 1

Diagnose Core
Challenges

Stage 1

Diagnose Core Challenges

 Uncover the real bottlenecks to growth

  • Identify the real bottleneck by choosing one critical business challenge such as sales optimization, delegation systems, profitability enhancement, or people management, and uncover its true root causes.

  • Use Balanced Scorecard diagnostic tools, founder interviews, and comprehensive assessments to identify the operational and strategic issues slowing down growth and innovation.

  • Map dashboards, review systems, SOPs, and founder time allocation to locate operational blockers and define practical, measurable outcomes achievable within 60–90 days.

Stage 2

Build Tactical
Solution

Stage 2

Build Tactical Solution

Design a custom playbook for execution

  • Apply EBAP frameworks including scorecards, ClickUp implementation, SOP creation, cash flow systems, JD development, and incentive design to address identified business challenges effectively.

  • Integrate learnings from all 9 modules into a comprehensive implementation playbook, combining strategy and operations to design scalable, actionable solutions.

  • Develop a  tracking dashboard with weekly review protocols, build team buy-in, and secure coach/CXO support to ensure alignment and smooth execution.

Stage 3

Implement &
Validate

Stage 3

Implement & Validate

Showcase measurable impact through execution

  • Execute the transformation plan within your company with structured faculty and mentor support, ensuring practical, on-ground implementation of designed solutions.

  • Troubleshoot real-time challenges through 1:1 coaching, founder clinics, and peer feedback, building resilience and adaptability during execution.

  • Achieve measurable business improvements such as enhanced collections, reduced founder dependency, increased lead generation, and present your Capstone journey with quantified results during the final residency.

How do I decide which programme to choose, or which programme is right for me?

If you are dealing with complex family dynamics or need long-term business transformation, leadership growth, and legacy building, OPM is the right choice. It is a 12-month programme for those focused on sustainable growth, succession, and global business exposure.

If there are not too many complications in your family dynamics, your primary pain point is sales or business acceleration, and you want to make a shorter commitment of your time, then 9-month BAP is a better program for you.

What is the main outcome difference?

  • 12 months OPM = holistic, sustainable transformation and leadership

  • 9 months BAP = fast, practical sales and process gains

Will there be the same level of rigour of practical application in both the programs?

Yes. Both programs include assignments and Capstone to ensure whatever is taught in the class is thoroughly applied in your company. A pressing problem that your organisation is currently facing is solved with the help of Capstone, with one on one coaching by faculty and mentors.

Will I miss out a lot by choosing the BAP?

It's all about what your need is. Yes, you will miss out on some aspects like in-depth modules for family, self, patriarch, non family CXOs. Also, international immersions and Fractional Board. Nevertheless, if your current pressing need is revenue growth, then you can go for BAP.

Is it worth doing the 12-month program if my goal is only sales growth?

It is better to compare all aspects of both programs, what different and additional you are getting in each, and then decide.

Can freshers apply for the 9-month BAP Certificate?

Not recommended. Ideal for those with at least 2–3 years of business/operations experience.

Is the 9-month BAP program also designed for family businesses?

Both OPM and BAP are designed keeping needs of family business owners and promoters.

Is the 9-month BAP course a subset of OPM?

No. It is a different program. While some sessions are similar to OPM, there are many sessions in BAP which are not taught in OPM. See Modules Comparison.

Can I switch from OPM to BAP?

OPM's first batch with generous scholarships, offer of bringing a family member for free, international immersions, Fractional Board, and almost 2X course modules offers incredible value. Hence, we strongly dissuade students from switching from OPM to BAP.

Having completed BAP, can I upgrade myself to OPM by doing additional courses and modules?

Yes, it is possible by doing additional modules and international immersions. However, we would like to understand your business objectives and then recommend the best way forward.

Can I upgrade from BAP to OPM during the program?

Yes, by completing additional modules and paying incremental fees. However, the total cost of upgrading through this pathway will be higher than pursuing OPM directly from the beginning. We recommend carefully evaluating your long term objectives before making this decision.

Can I bring a family member or my professional team to the 9-month BAP program?

No. This benefit is exclusively available for the first cohort of 12-month OPM.

Is the business simulation/ personal interview conducted online or offline?

The in-person business simulation and interview will be conducted on the same day for a student. These will be scheduled across major cities (Delhi, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Indore, Hyderabad, Chandigarh) in India and over Zoom in exceptional cases.

What is GD or business simulation ? How to prepare for it?

The in-person business simulation will require students to role-play as a CXO of a company and solve its business challenges in a team of 5-6 applicants. We will send them a case study to prepare for the business simulation round 3-4 days prior for preparation. In this round, they will be judged on their creativity, communication, and teamwork skills. They will be participating in a group discussion for 45 minutes followed by a presentation (without any slides).

What happens in the personal interview, what kind of questions will be asked?

The interview will be a one-on-one conversation with our faculty or senior leadership. The questions will be around their application and motivations for applying to Masters' Union. The goal is to know more about students and what they want to achieve at Masters' Union.

Is there any detailed information about GD/PI ?

  • The overall process will take around 3 hours.

  • The business simulation will last 45 minutes followed by a 15 minute group presentation. Students will be working in teams of 4-6 students on a simulated business scenario, where students will be expected to make decisions, analyse data and present their findings.

  • The interview will be conducted by a senior leader or a faculty member at Masters’ Union and will be approximately 30 minutes long. They will be asked questions about their application including their achievements, essays and the video essay submitted.

  • Dress code for the event is smart casuals.

When will the results be declared?

The results are announced 30-45 days after the date of the interview. You can find more details here.

Is there a firm deadline for payment of the admission fee?

We provide students ample time till July of the academic year so that they can make their decision based on all the admission offers that they have. In case they still want an extension, the same can be granted based on an email request.

What is the mode of payment for the fees?

Mode of payment can be online, net banking, bank transfer or in some rare cases they can even send a cheque. Except for cash, we accept all payment methods.

What is the fee payment schedule?

Students can pay the fees semester wise. Starting every semester, they will be notified about the fees due by ugadmissions@mastersunion.org

Is the study abroad programme included in the fee? Is there a different fee?

The study abroad programme is not included in the standard tuition fee. Students opting for the 3+1 Dual Degree UG Programme will need to pay tuition fee as per Illinois Tech & living expenses of Chicago.

Does Masters’ Union offer scholarships?

At Masters’ Union we believe that financial constraints should not pose any hindrance to students’ academic aspirations. We have merit and need-based scholarships for all deserving candidates. Students scoring above 35 out of 45 marks in the Masters’ Union Scholarship Aptitude test get a scholarship which can range from 10% - 25% of the tuition fee depending on the student’s performance in the Scholarship (Aptitude)Test and the overall profile of the applicant. For candidates keen on scholarship, we encourage students to submit their application early to allow for our admission counsellors to thoroughly review their scholarship case before presenting it to the admissions committee at the time of evaluation.

Is the scholarship for all four years or just for the first year?

Scholarship is awarded for all four years. If a student receives a 20% scholarship, they will receive a 20% scholarship every semester on their respective fees. However, in cases of misconduct or rule violations, disciplinary action may be taken.

Is the Masters’ Union scholarship applicable to the Illinois Tech fee?

No, the Masters’ Union scholarship is not applicable to the Illinois Tech fee. However, students nominated and admitted to Illinois Tech through the 3+1 Dual Degree UG Programme may be eligible for a merit-based scholarship of up to 25% of tuition fees.

To qualify, students must maintain a cumulative GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher. The final decision on scholarship awards and eligibility is determined by Illinois Tech in accordance with its admission and scholarship policies.

Are there any financing options?

Yes, there are financial institutions that can provide loans for up to 95% of the programme fees to the admitted students. Read here for further details.

Is the admissions process different for the 3+1 Dual Degree UG Programme with Illinois Institute of Technology?

No, the admissions process for the 3+1 Dual Degree UG Programme is the same as the standard process. Applicants must apply through the regular admissions portal, and eligibility for the programme is assessed based on their academic performance, extracurricular activities, and an interview.

Will students have the option to choose the duration of transfer?

Currently, the programme is designed as a one-year transfer option. Students will spend their fourth year at Illinois Institute of Technology, completing the final year of their undergraduate degree as part of the 3+1 Dual Degree UG Programme.

When can the student decide if they want to opt for the 3+1 Dual Degree UG Programme with Illinois Institute of Technology?

Students are strongly encouraged to decide on opting for the 3+1 Dual Degree UG Programme before starting their undergraduate journey at Masters’ Union. Early confirmation ensures guaranteed seat allocation and allows for streamlined academic planning tailored to the programme's requirements.

While students may still choose to opt for the programme during their third year, this is subject to seat availability, which will be communicated at that time. Confirming at the beginning ensures a smooth transition and optimal preparation for their transfer to Illinois Tech.

Will the curriculum at Illinois Tech align with what students study at Masters’ Union?

Yes, the curriculum has been designed to ensure a seamless transfer between the institutions. Students will continue to build on the foundational and advanced topics they study at Masters’ Union.

Can students secure internships in the US while at Illinois Tech?

Yes, students will have access to Illinois Tech’s career services, which include internship placements, job fairs, and networking events with global employers.

What is the structure of the IIT programme?

The IIT programme of Masters' Union's UG Programme in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence gives the student an option to enrol in IIT Guwahati's online Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence Check Programme.

What are the benefits of this programme?

In this programme you gain a comprehensive education in data science and artificial intelligence from two esteemed institutions, Masters’ Union and IIT Guwahati. Along with Masters’ Union, students get access to IIT’s industry-oriented curriculum, covering advanced topics and providing hands-on experience with high-performance computing facilities.

What is the fee structure and who pays for it?

The fee structure for the programme is available on our website. Students pay a single fee to Masters' Union for the entire 4-year Undergraduate Programme in Data Science & AI, which includes the IIT programme fees. Masters' Union also handles all registration and administrative processes for the chosen IIT, ensuring a hassle-free experience for students.

Why should I not enrol directly in IIT Guwahati without Masters' Union?

Enrolling through the collaboration with Masters' Union offers several unique benefits:

  • Industry Immersion: Spend 3 Years at Masters' Union, followed by a one-year industry immersion at top tech giants in India, providing you with invaluable hands-on experience.

  • Dual Advantage & Professional Certificate: You get parallel enrollment in the Bachelor of Science at IIT Guwahati, and also receive a Professional Certificate for the Undergraduate Programme in Data Science & AI from Masters' Union, enhancing your credentials.

  • Placement Opportunities: Masters' Union’s strong ties with marquee recruiters like Microsoft, Google, PwC, and Krutrim increase your chances of securing top placements.

Can I get both the Illinois Tech degree and the IIT degree?

Yes, with the option to enrol in Bachelor of Science at IIT Guwahati, you can pursue the degree for 3 years and spend your 4th year at Illinois Tech, Chicago, earning a second degree — a Bachelor of Science in AI. This pathway allows you to graduate with two prestigious degrees in 4 years, along with global exposure and placement opportunities in the US.

Alternatively, if you choose to complete all 4 years in India, you spend your final year immersed in a hands-on industry internship, gaining practical experience with top tech companies.
