About Agnel Institute of Technology and Design (AITD)

AITD is a beacon of excellence in Assagao, Goa, offering transformative undergraduate and MBA programs. We integrate rigorous academic theory with hands-on, skill-based training through state-of-the-art labs and ICT infrastructure, empowering students to bridge the gap between classroom concepts and real-world innovation.

Vision & Infrastructure

Experience modern laboratory facilities and a campus culture that fosters research, innovation, and active learning.

Mission

Providing affordable, world-class education and complete placement support to shape the visionary leaders of tomorrow.

Academic Excellence

Guided by expert faculty and deep-rooted industry exposure to turn academic honors into long-term professional success.

Mission & Placement

Shapes future-ready professionals and leaders with affordable world-class education and strong placement support.
