Bharat Summer Fellowship

A 3,000 km journey across 10+ cities, blending on-ground immersions with CXO-led
insights and hands-on business challenges.

Download Brochure

Explore 10+ Cities and Learn through
20+ Immersions

Hub 1

Delhi NCR

Explore Delhi’s retail heritage, airport cargo logistics, Parliament, Makers’ Lab & understand luxury positioning at Mercedes-Benz dealership and learn from CXOs.

Hub 2

Dehradun

Explore manufacturing excellence at SIDCUL Haridwar belt, visit Forest Research Institute and understand pharma manufacturing in the Selaqui industrial area.

Hub 3

Jalandhar

Explore large-scale manufacturing at ITC, global brand-building at NIVIA, CXO insights and 650-acre education & agri ecosystems at LPU and a Fellowship sports day.

Hub 4

Jaipur

Explore the gems industry, local markets, royal heritage and innovative business models at CARS24 and Jaipur Rugs.

Hub 4

Mundra & Ahmedabad

Explore 3 Adani enterprises covering Ports, Wilmar, and Solar, tour Amul's dairy factory, engage with IIM Ahmedabad Ventures, and attend 4 CXO sessions.

Hub 5

Mumbai

Explore production house operations, experience Bollywood tour, understand the Dabbawala network and earn a Bombay Stock Exchange certification.

Hub 6

Goa

Cruise to Goa on Cordelia, explore iron mines and a Feni distillery, and immerse in Fado music, Portuguese culture, and Goa's nightlife.

Hub 6

Bengaluru

Learn e-commerce at Meesho, explore EV and emerging tech and dive into design thinking at a Renovation Lab.

Hub 8

Darjeeling

Experience a rural immersion in Indo-Nepal villages and visit Darjeeling tea estates to learn brand building, culture, and sustainability.

Hub 9

Lucknow

Explore Uttar Pradesh's defense manufacturing, global luxury embroidery, and meet government leaders shaping a trillion-dollar economy.

The immersions listed are indicative and may change based on partner availability and unforeseen circumstances.

Inside India's Top Businesses with Masters' Union

Factory visits, CXO chats, workshop visits and boardroom discussions. This is what
student business immersion and learning looks like in Bharat Summer Fellowship.

Who is Bharat Summer Fellowship for?

    The Bharat Summer Fellowship is for learners aged 18–21 who learn best by
    travelling and doing.

    This programme is ideal for you if you are:

  • Curious about the nuances and depth of India’s businesses and cultures

  • Comfortable with intensive travel, new environments and experiential learning

  • Believe in hands-on learning beyond classrooms

  • Open to self-discovery through real-world exposure

What is Bharat Summer Fellowship?

Bharat Summer Fellowship is a 6 week immersion-led programme that uses India as the classroom, combining travel with business and cultural immersions, and one day challenges for hands-on learning.

How many cities and immersions does the programme cover?

Students travel across 10+ cities and engage in 20+ immersions experiences covering industries, enterprises and cultural sites.

What is a fellowship?

Bharat Summer Fellowship is an immersion-led learning programme that offers participants structured experiential exposure through travel across India, business and cultural immersions and hands-on projects in the form of One Day Challenges.

Will I get a degree after completing Bharat Summer Fellowship?

You will receive a fellowship certification.

What will I learn in the programme?

Through industry immersions, CXO interactions, and cultural experiences, you will gain first-hand exposure to how businesses operate across India. You can build a digital identity through Content Creation and sharpen entrepreneurial thinking through the One Day Challenges.

What is the programme structure?

  • Duration: 6 weeks

  • 2 Day Orientation at the Gurugram campus

  • Weeks 1 to Week 6: Travel-based business and cultural immersions across India

  • Ongoing One Day Challenges

What is the eligibility criteria?

For students aged 18–21 currently pursuing an undergraduate degree, or who have completed or are completing Class 12 this year.

Do I need prior work experience?

No prior work experience is required. The programme is designed for learners at different stages of their journey, but it is especially recommended for those with a strong interest in business, travel and experiential learning.

How do I apply?

You can apply by using Apply Now here, fill out the online application form and start your transformative journey across India.

Are scholarships available for Bharat Summer Fellowship?

Yes, we offer 5 types of scholarships to support deserving students from different backgrounds and communities. You can explore the details of each scholarship on our Admissions page.

How can I avail a scholarship for Bharat Summer Fellowship?

When filling out your application, you can indicate interest in applying for a scholarship. Shortlisted candidates will be asked to provide supporting documents or essays to demonstrate eligibility for specific scholarships.

Is there any age limit to apply for the programme?

The programme is most suitable for learners between 18 and 21 years of age who want to combine business learning with travel and immersive experiences.

Will I get to network with industry leaders?

Yes! You will learn and engage with 10+ CXOs and mentors from Fortune 500 companies, top unicorns and emerging startups across India.

Will I get cultural exposure while learning business?

Yes, cultural immersions are integrated at every city stop, including heritage sites, local communities and regional business practices.

What is a Million Dollar Portfolio?

As you take part in 12+ business immersions with leading companies, you’ll build a portfolio through hands-on assignments and real evaluations by senior leaders. This becomes your Million Dollar Portfolio, which is proof of the skills and experiences you’ve gained.

What are One Day Challenges?

One Day Challenges are hands-on activities organised each week where students earn real revenue, create marketing campaigns, explore how consulting works, and build core business skills during the programme.

How will the learning outcomes be assessed?

Your progress will be evaluated through One Day Challenges and active participation throughout the programme. Assessments are designed in collaboration with senior leaders, with feedback and evaluations coming directly from them.

Will the programme help with placements or internships?

Bharat Summer Fellowship does not guarantee placements or internships. However, students may gain access to real opportunities through networking with CXOs, collaborating with partner companies and showcasing their Million Dollar Portfolio.

How many students will be in the cohort?

Each cohort will have ~60 students, ensuring close interactions with mentors and meaningful peer-to-peer learning.

What kind of peer group can I expect?

Students come from diverse academic backgrounds, ranging from engineering and business to arts and social sciences, united by a passion for business, travel and immersive learning.

Who teaches or mentors in the programme?

The faculty includes CXOs, founders and industry leaders across sectors. You’ll also be mentored directly by 10+ executives from Fortune 500 companies, unicorns and startups during immersions.

How does the programme ensure decorum and discipline?

We maintain a clear code of conduct, provide structured schedules and have dedicated male and female programme managers accompanying the cohort. This ensures a safe, respectful and professional environment throughout all travel, company visits and cultural immersions.

How does the programme ensure decorum and discipline?

We maintain a clear code of conduct, provide structured schedules and have dedicated male and female programme managers accompanying the cohort. This ensures a safe, respectful and professional environment throughout all travel, company visits and cultural immersions.

What does the programme fee include?

The fee covers all core programme expenses. This includes inter-city travel post orientation, local transport, meals and shared accommodation at each location. It also covers access to immersions, workshops and learning resources provided during the journey. Personal expenses such as shopping, leisure activities or additional purchases are not included.

What kind of accommodation will be provided?

Students stay in safe, secure, shared accommodations, typically twin-sharing rooms in hotels, hostels or serviced residences arranged by Masters’ Union in each city.

Does the programme include travel insurance and safety measures?

Yes, every student is covered under travel insurance. Safety is our top priority, and we work with certified professionals dedicated to ensuring the well-being of all participants throughout the journey.
